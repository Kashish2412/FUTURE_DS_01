TASK1:
Superstore Sales & Returns Dashboard (2011–2014)
This Power BI dashboard provides a detailed analysis of the Superstore dataset from 2011 to 2014, focusing on sales performance, profitability, product returns, and category-wise insights.
The dashboard is designed to help business teams identify high-performing products, monitor yearly trends, and understand customer return behavior.
Dashboard Highlights
✔ Yearly Trends

Line chart showing yearly growth in Sales and Profit.

✔ Best-Selling Products (Top 10)

Horizontal bar chart ranking the top revenue-generating products.

✔ Category Sales Distribution

Donut chart showing performance of:

Office Supplies

Technology

Furniture

✔ Sub-Category Sales

Bar chart revealing high and low-performing sub-categories.

✔ Region Filter

Slicer to analyze regional performance:

East

West

Central

South
